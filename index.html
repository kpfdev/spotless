Welcome to Spotless!

Spotless is a project by KPF Urban Interface (KPFui) that quantifies the potential of off-street parking. Explore New York City's existing parking in "parking viewer" mode, and discover the impacts of redeveloping these parking lots in "build mode".

With autonomous vehicles (AVs) on the horizon, some people speculate New York City won't need as much parking in the future.

What if we made every parking lot a park?

There would be 8 thousand acres of new park in NYC, or almost 8 new Central Parks.

What if we build housing on every parking lot?

There would be 3.1 million new apartments in NYC. That would almost double the housing stock!
